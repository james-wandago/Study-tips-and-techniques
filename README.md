## Google search as AI tool
Google is heavily involved in Ai learning tools by providing, developing, and intergrating generative AI technology into educational, professional, and developer platforms.
its role spans from providing conversational learning companions to offering advanced cloud-based training for machine learning
### How google is used as a tool in AI learning
1. **Interactive learning and personalized study**
   *Gemini*(Guided learning) provides step-by-step explanations, quizzes, and multimedia responses
   *Gemini live and vision*: Facilitates voice-based,real-time problem-solving and uses camera inputs (google lens) for analysis
   *NotebookLM*: Generates summaries, study guides, flashcards,and audio overviews from uploaded materials like PDFs and documents
   **Development and technical training(vertex AI & AI studio)**
   A platform for prototyping and testing machine learning models, allowing users to experiment with generative AI and develop AIagents without needindgadvanced technical skills
   **Google AI & Cloud learning**
   provides curated tutorials,"prompting essentials" training and guides on foundational AI concepts to help users build practical skills.
   **Education AI Tools**
   offers tailored AI tools to assist educators in planning lessons and enhancing student learning experiences, while also focusing on responsible AI usage
