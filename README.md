## Recap

**Written by: Mitchel**

The best study results come from combining multiple study techniques instead of relying on just one. Techniques like **short breaks**, **active recall**, and **mind mapping** work best when used together.

### Why Recapping Is Important
- Helps summarize what you have learned
- Reinforces memory and understanding
- Helps identify areas that need more practice
- Improves long-term retention of information

### How to Practice Recap
1. After studying a topic, pause and summarize the main ideas in your own words.
2. Write down key points without looking at your notes.
3. Compare your recap with the original material.
4. Repeat the process regularly to strengthen memory.

> A short recap after every study session can significantly improve how much information you retain.

## Class Discussion

Class discussion is an interactive study method where students actively participate by sharing ideas, asking questions, and debating topics in a group setting. It helps improve understanding because students explain concepts in their own words and learn from different perspectives.

### Why It Is Effective
- Encourages critical thinking
- Improves communication skills
- Helps students remember information better
- Builds confidence in speaking

### How to Use It Effectively
- Prepare before class by reading the material
- Listen carefully to others’ opinions
- Ask questions if something is unclear
- Take notes during the discussion
