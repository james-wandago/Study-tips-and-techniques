## Recap
**Written by: Mitchel**

The best study results come from combining multiple study techniques instead of relying on just one. Techniques like **short breaks**, **active recall**, **mind mapping**, **effective Google searching**, and **class discussions** work best when used together.

Using a mix of strategies helps students stay focused, understand concepts more deeply, and remember information for longer periods.

### Why Recapping is Important
- Reinforces what you have learned.
- Helps identify areas you did not fully understand.
- Improves long-term memory retention.
- Connects different study techniques together.

### Best Way to Recap Your Learning
1. Review the key points from your study session.
2. Summarize the information in your own words.
3. Test yourself using **active recall**.
4. Discuss difficult concepts with classmates.
5. Use **mind maps** or notes to visualize connections.

### Final Thought
Studying effectively is not about studying longer — it is about studying **smarter**. By combining these techniques and regularly recapping what you learn, students can greatly improve their understanding and academic performance.
