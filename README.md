## Recap
**Written by: Mitchel**

> **Key Idea:** The best study results come from combining multiple study techniques rather than relying on just one.

The most effective learning happens when students combine different study methods. Techniques like **short breaks**, **active recall**, **mind mapping**, **Google search for research**, and **class discussions** complement each other and strengthen understanding.

### Why Recapping is Important
- Reinforces what you have learned
- Helps identify knowledge gaps
- Improves long-term memory retention
- Connects ideas from different study techniques

### Best Way to Recap Your Learning
1. Review the key points from your study session.
2. Summarize the information in your own words.
3. Test yourself using **active recall**.
4. Discuss challenging concepts with classmates.
5. Use **mind maps** to visualize relationships between ideas.

### Quick Recap Checklist
- [ ] Review notes
- [ ] Summarize key ideas
- [ ] Self-test using active recall
- [ ] Discuss with classmates
- [ ] Create a simple mind map

### Final Thought
Studying effectively is not about studying longer — it is about studying **smarter**. Combining multiple study techniques and regularly recapping what you learn can significantly improve academic performance.
